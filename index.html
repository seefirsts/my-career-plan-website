About the Job

An aviation inspector is responsible for ensuring that aircraft are safe to fly by inspecting them for compliance with regulations and standards set by aviation authorities.

Responsibilities

  • Conducting pre-flight and post-flight inspections
  • Reviewing aircraft maintenance records
  • Investigating aircraft accidents and incidents
  • Issuing airworthiness certificates
  • Ensuring compliance with safety regulations

Requirements

  • Valid aviation inspector license
  • Experience in aircraft maintenance or inspection
  • Knowledge of aviation regulations
  • Attention to detail
  • Good communication skills
